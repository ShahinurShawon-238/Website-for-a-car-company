The preservation and restoration of automobiles is the mechanical or cosmetic repair of cars. Restoration means removing, replacing, or repairing the parts of a vehicle, while preservation means keeping the original components. Though automotive restoration is commonly defined as the reconditioning of a vehicle from an original condition in an effort to return it to like-new or better condition, There are many styles of which a vehicle can be restored, any of which can be performed at the discretion, desire, or taste of a vehicle owner or restorer.
